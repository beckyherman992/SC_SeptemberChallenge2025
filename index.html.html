Why Portugal?

My dream to visit Portugal is deeply personal. It's the land where some of my family roots are, and I've always been fascinated by its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and language. From the historic streets of Lisbon to the picturesque beaches of the Algarve, Portugal offers a unique blend of history, nature, and modernity that I can't wait to explore. It's the country where my mom was born, specifically on the beautiful island of Terceira in the Azores. Learning Portuguese has made me feel even more connected to this incredible place. I want to walk the same streets she did and discover the roots of my family.
